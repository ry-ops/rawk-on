Rawk On — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-26

What we collect

Rawk On stores the following locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local:

  • Your TIDAL Client ID (entered by you in Settings)
  • Your TIDAL OAuth access and refresh tokens, obtained when you log in
  • A short-lived cache of playlist IDs to avoid duplicate adds

None of this data ever leaves your device to our servers — we have no servers.

What we don't collect

Rawk On does not collect analytics, crash reports, or any personally identifiable information. It contains no tracking scripts.

Third-party services

The extension communicates directly with:

  • TIDAL (openapi.tidal.com, auth.tidal.com, login.tidal.com) — to authenticate and manage your playlists. TIDAL's own privacy policy applies to that data.
  • The Current (www.thecurrent.org) — only to read the playlist page you are already viewing. No data is sent to The Current.

Permissions

  • storage — to persist your Client ID and OAuth tokens locally
  • identity — to open the TIDAL OAuth login window via Chrome's identity API
